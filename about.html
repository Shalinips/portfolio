How I Think & Work

Problem First

I focus on understanding the problem clearly before choosing tools or writing code. A well-defined problem leads to simpler and more effective solutions.

Build → Test → Improve

I prefer building functional versions early, testing them in real scenarios, and improving step by step instead of aiming for perfection at once.

Learning by Doing

I learn best by experimenting, fixing mistakes, and understanding why a solution works, not just that it works.
